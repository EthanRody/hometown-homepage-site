Top three activities to do in Kitcher

View the fascinating natural world

Visit the Elora Gorge for activities such as camping, swimming, and canoeing.

Peruse the shops

The St. Jacobs Farmers Market is an interesting place with an interesting collection of small shops to peruse at your leisure

Enjoy untouched nature

Go to the Rockwood Preservation Area to view Nature in its untouched state on a grand hike.

If you come in Winter be prepared for snow, ice, and the cold!

Some of the above mentioned activities may be unavailable in the winter season
